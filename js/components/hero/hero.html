Your Bridge to Global Education and Personal Growth Journey

Bayan International Group for University Services and Training is a Lebanese organization dedicated to university-related matters. It serves as a representative for multiple Arab and foreign universities, aiming to streamline the admission process for students aspiring to pursue education across different academic tiers (Bachelor's, Master's, Doctorate).

The organization extends academic and administrative assistance to these students. Furthermore, it actively engages in diverse training initiatives encompassing areas such as administration, industry, and personal development.
